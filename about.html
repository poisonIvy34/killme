A Little About Me

(and my crazy life)

Hi, I'm Ella. I am 18 and I am from all over the place. My home now is Utah but I spent most of my childhood in the UAE! I love it there and it is where I consider I am from. Because I lived in the UAE I had the opportunity to travel to many beautiful countries. Which sounds extravagant and it was amazing but because we were so close to so many countries it was not as big of an excursion as it sounds. I have also lived in Indiana, New York, and now Utah. All together I have visited four continents, lived in two and been to 7+ countries!

I LOVE learning and pushing myslef. It is so satisfying to complete something you thought you couldn't do! I am currently studying at Brigham Young University. I have not selected a major but I will apply to Experience Design and Management and Information Systems this spring. I enjoy both areas of study and think I could be happy in either one so it will be hard to decide if I get in both of them.

Outside of school I love art and music. I also love spending time outside doing pretty much anything. Backpacking, rock climbing, swimming, you name it and I'll love it.
